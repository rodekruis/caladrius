Caladrius should only be used with the intention to provide useful information to disaster aid workers.

Caladrius should be used in adherence to the 7 fundamental principles of the Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement,
1. Humanity
2. Impartiality
3. Neutrality
4. Independence
5. Voluntary Service
6. Unity
7. Universality

For more information on these principles, please visit https://media.ifrc.org/ifrc/who-we-are/fundamental-principles/

For the privacy statement of the Dutch Red Cross, please visit https://www.rodekruis.nl/privacy-statement/

For all other information, including contact details, please visit https://www.rodekruis.nl/
